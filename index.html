The Basic Package

We'll move your stuff into your dorm and get you situated.

The basic package provides all the manual labor required to help you move in. We offer services such as carting your luggage from one location to another, as well as unloading services. Essentially, we take the move-in aspect off your hands, and ensure that your belongings arrive at your residence a stress-free fashion.

The Plus Package

You get a car and a mover to help.

The plus package includes all the features of the basic package, with the additional of our trusted driving service. If you are moving from residence to another, or retrieving your belongings from a storage space, we will provide you with an appropriate vehicle for transportation. And unlike any other moving service, this includes an extra set of helping hands to make sure you’re all settled in afterwards.

The Premium Package

A big car and twice the manpower.

The premium package includes the basic and plus package features but also contains our VIP service of helping you unpack. Under your direction, we will help you unpack your luggage after we’ve transported them to your residence. With our help, you’ll no longer experience that dreaded feeling associated with having to unpack your things.
